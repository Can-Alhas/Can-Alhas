### About me

- I'm interested in many areas of **computer science**, but my main focus is **game development**.
- Focused on **engine architecture**, **graphics/rendering**, and **runtime systems**.
- I enjoy building low-level systems around **memory**, **platform layers**, **tools**, and **performance**.
- Also interested in **reverse engineering**, **emulators**, **graphics APIs**, and how software works under the hood.
- Working with **C++**, **Unreal Engine**, **Godot**, **Go**, **SDL**, **Qt**, and **wxWidgets**.
